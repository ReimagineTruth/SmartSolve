Help Center

Find answers to common questions or contact our support team.

How do I get started with SmartSolve?

Sign up using your Pi Wallet on the sign-in page. You can start with the free plan or choose a paid plan from the pricing page.

Why can’t I sign in?

Ensure you have a Pi Wallet set up. If you’re having issues, check our Pi Wallet guide or contact support via the form below.

How do I upgrade my plan?

After signing in, go to your account settings or visit the pricing page to select a new plan. Payments are processed securely with Pi.

What if I forget my Pi Wallet credentials?

Visit the Pi Network’s official site to recover your wallet. SmartSolve uses Pi SDK for authentication, so your credentials are managed by Pi.
