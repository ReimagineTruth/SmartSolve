Your Pi Wallet

Learn how to set up and use your Pi Wallet to unlock SmartSolve’s features.

What is a Pi Wallet?

A Pi Wallet is your secure digital wallet for the Pi Network, allowing you to store, send, and receive Pi cryptocurrency. SmartSolve uses your Pi Wallet for seamless sign-in and payments.

How to Set Up Your Pi Wallet

Follow these steps to get started with your Pi Wallet:

  • Download the Pi Network app from the Pi Network website or your app store.
  • Create an account or sign in using your Pi credentials.
  • Complete the KYC (Know Your Customer) process to verify your identity.
  • Enable your Pi Wallet in the app to start using Pi for transactions.
  • Visit SmartSolve’s sign-in page to authenticate with your Pi Wallet.

Using Your Pi Wallet with SmartSolve

Your Pi Wallet integrates with SmartSolve to:

  • Sign in securely using Pi SDK authentication.
  • Pay for Standard, Premium, or Pro plans with Pi.
  • Access local services within the Pi ecosystem.
  • Track your Pi transactions in the Budget & Finance Management feature.

Need help? Visit our Help Center or contact support at support@smartsolve.app.
