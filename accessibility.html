Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making SmartSolve accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment

SmartSolve strives to ensure that our platform is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following features to enhance accessibility:

  • Keyboard navigation support for all interactive elements.
  • Screen reader compatibility with ARIA landmarks and labels.
  • High-contrast and dark mode options for users with visual impairments.
  • Responsive design to ensure usability on various devices and screen sizes.
  • Skip links to allow users to bypass repetitive navigation.

Third-Party Content

SmartSolve integrates with the Pi Network for authentication and payments. While we strive to ensure accessibility, some third-party content (e.g., Pi SDK interfaces) may not fully meet WCAG standards. We are working with our partners to improve accessibility.

Feedback and Accommodations

If you encounter accessibility barriers or need accommodations, please contact us at accessibility@smartsolve.app. We are dedicated to addressing your concerns promptly.

You can also submit feedback via our Contact Page or visit our Help Center for additional support.

Ongoing Efforts

We regularly test our platform with assistive technologies and conduct accessibility audits to identify and fix issues. Our team is committed to continuous improvement to ensure an inclusive experience for all users.
