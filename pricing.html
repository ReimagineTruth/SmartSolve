Pricing FAQs

Answers to common questions about our plans.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

The free plan is a great way to try SmartSolve. Paid plans don’t have a separate trial, but you can cancel within 7 days for a full refund.

How do I pay with Pi?

Payments are processed securely through your Pi Wallet. After selecting a plan, you’ll be prompted to authorize the transaction via the Pi SDK.
